# Light Map Icon Brainstorming

## Objective
Explore design ideas for an official Light Map application icon that represents AR tabletop gaming, projectors, hands, and the merging of physical and digital worlds.

## Design Themes

### 1. The Interaction (Hand + Projection)
- **Concept**: A hand silhouette reaching into a glowing, projected map grid.
- **Symbolism**: Human-centric interaction, tactile feel, and digital enhancement.
- **Aesthetic**: Vibrant blues and purples, soft glows, sharp grid lines.
- **Images**:
  - `nanobanana-output/a_modern_app_icon_for_light_map__1.png` (Original concept)
  - `nanobanana-output/a_modern_app_icon_for_light_map__2.png` (Original concept)
  - `nanobanana-output/editisolate_the_central_app_icon.png` (Refined, isolated version)

### 2. The Beam (Light + Surface)
- **Concept**: A stylized light beam emitted from a projector hitting a flat surface and "splitting" into a complex map or grid.
- **Symbolism**: Precision, transformation, and the bridge between hardware (projector) and software (map).
- **Aesthetic**: Geometric, high-contrast, beams of light, sharp focal points.

### 3. Minimalist "L" / "LM"
- **Concept**: A minimalist representation of the letters 'L' or 'LM' constructed from glowing light paths or a stylized corner of a gaming table.
- **Symbolism**: Modern, recognizable, efficient.
- **Aesthetic**: Sharp lines, cyan on dark blue, minimalist flat design.
- **Images**:
  - `nanobanana-output/minimalist_app_icon_for_light_ma.png`

## Next Steps
1. Review generated concepts with stakeholders.
2. Select a primary direction for refinement.
3. Generate high-resolution versions and various sizes (favicon, mobile app icon, etc.).
